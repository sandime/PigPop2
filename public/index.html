Welcome to Piggington Beach by the Sea, home to Pig Popular and his friends

Pig Popular loves to skateboard. He’s also the surfing champion of Piggington Beach. But when Mimsy Sage Pig moves to town, life changes for Pig Popular. Mimsy Sage, who grew up surfing with the great Pig Duke, is the surfing champ of her hometown -- and she never misses an opportunity to brag about it. What’s worse, Pig Popular’s best friend Wooten is spending a lot more time with Mimsy Sage. So are the rest of the pigs, who are dressing and even speaking like her. You could call her a trendsetter. What will happen when Pig Popular and Mimsy Sage face off in the annual Piggington Grand surf contest?
Pig Popular is a chapter book, suitable for children in grades 1-5; ages 5 to 10. Similar books include Winnie-the-Pooh, and the Geronimo Stilton and Junie B. Jones series.
